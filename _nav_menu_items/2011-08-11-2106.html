This way you could de-enqueue it if you so desired (most plugins I’ve seen out there write script code directly to wp_head). This plugin also adds Bird Feeder tracking into to your feeds (props nickforge). I’ve grouped all three feeds (RSS, RSS2, Atom) into one single name since I really don’t care which feed people are using. Download available at http://devin.reams.me/projects/minty/ (hosted at WordPress.org) *Last updated: May 14, 2011*
