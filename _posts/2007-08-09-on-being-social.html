--- layout: post title: On Being Social tags: - Internet - People status: publish type: post published: true meta: btc_comment_counts: a:0:{} --- In the last few months I've become close friends with some people very quickly. At first it seems odd that you can connect so quickly and be so comfortable with people; I've learned the trick! Become well versed in the following: sports, music, and movies (not TV shows, there are just too many). You can always start a (recent, topical) conversation and carry it into other interests.
