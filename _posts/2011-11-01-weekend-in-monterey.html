This past weekend Rachel and I visited a friend in Monterey to get away from the sudden snow in Denver and hang out on beaches (that really wasn't the reason, but a nice side-effect). We did a bike ride along 17 Mile, visited Pebble Beach, saw excellent sunsets, made a bonfire on the beach, learned how to surf, visited vineyards in Carmel, drove down to Big Sur, and had plenty of great meals.

"I can see why people live in California."
